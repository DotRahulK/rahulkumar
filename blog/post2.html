When your application starts to groan under its own weight, it's time to rethink how it's structured. In this article, I share the journey of splitting a monolithic backend API into modular, independently deployable services.

We began by identifying clear domain boundaries and then extracted each domain into its own NestJS module and service. By doing so, we could scale the pieces independently, reduce deployment risk and improve developer productivity. The result? A 25¬†% improvement in average API response times and a codebase that's far easier to evolve.

Along the way we discovered some gotchas with shared data models and authentication, and I'll dive into how we addressed those challenges in a future post.
